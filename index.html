Time Capsule

Time Capsule is a project that allows you to send a message to the future. You can write a letter to yourself or to someone else and set a date for it to be delivered. The message will be stored in a secure location and will be delivered on the date you specify. You can also choose to keep the message private or make it public. If you choose to make it public, it will be displayed on the Time Capsule website for others to read. You can also choose to receive a notification when the message is delivered. Time Capsule is a fun and unique way to send a message to the future and share your thoughts with others.

How it works

Time Capsule is easy to use. Simply go to the Time Capsule website and click on the "Create a Capsule" button.

You will be prompted to enter your name, email address, and the message you want to send. You can also choose to make the message public or private, set a delivery date, and choose to receive a notification when the message is delivered.

Once you have entered all the information, click on the "Send" button and your message will be stored in a secure location. When the delivery date arrives, your message will be sent to the recipient and you will receive a notification if you chose to do so.

You can also view your message on the Time Capsule website and share it with others.
